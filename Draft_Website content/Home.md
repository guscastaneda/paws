## THE DIFFERENCE SECTION

### Why Paws on Longmeadow?

There are a lot of places to leave your dog. What sets us apart isn't just what we do. It's where and how we do it.

Paws on Longmeadow is based in a colonial home on a quiet street in Sharon, MA. This is where Gus and Marian live, where their boys are growing up, and where Ollie has spent his whole life surrounded by dogs who needed a place to belong. At 9 years old, our founding member and resident authority still greets every new guest like it's his personal responsibility.

When your dog stays with us, they get the warmth of a family home and the confidence of a team that has been doing this professionally since 2018.

## SERVICES SECTION

### What We Offer

Every service is provided in or around our home in Sharon, MA.

🏡 **Boarding** — Your dog stays overnight with us, in our home. Full access to the house, the yard, and the family.

☀️ **Full-Day Daycare** — A full day of play, socialization, and supervised fun. Drop off in the morning, pick up in the evening, tired and happy.

🌤️ **Half-Day Daycare** — All the good stuff, half the day. Perfect for dogs who need a social outlet without a full-day commitment.

🏠 **House Sitting** *(by request)* — We come to you. Your dog stays in their own home, on their own schedule, with their own routines intact.

🐾 **Drop-In Visit** *(by request)* — A scheduled check-in at your home covering feeding, play, and a potty break. Great for busy days when your dog just needs a little company.

🦮 **Group Walk** *(by request)* — Fresh air, good company, and real exercise. Your dog gets out into the world with a small group and comes home ready to rest.

🚗 **Pet Taxi** *(by request)* — We'll pick up or drop off your pup so you don't have to rearrange your day. Available for daycare, vet visits, grooming appointments and more.
