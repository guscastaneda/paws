## Every Dog Has a Story Here

Some days it is a first-timer figuring out the yard. Some days it is a regular who has claimed the same spot on the couch for the third week in a row. Some days it is two dogs who met on a Monday and became inseparable by Friday.

## Behind the Camera

Fair warning: most of the good photos here are Marian's. Gus takes photos too. The difference, by everyone's admission, is almost comical.

Marian has an eye for the moment just before a dog does something ridiculous, joyful, or both. Every image on this page was taken by someone who genuinely could not help herself.

## What You Will Find Here

**Photos** of the dogs in our care, on Ollie's Deck, in the yard, on the couch, mid-zoomie, mid-nap, and every state in between.

**Videos** of group play, arrivals, walks, and the kind of unscripted moments that make this work worth doing.

**Client stories** from families who trusted us with their dogs and wanted to share what that experience was like. In their own words.
