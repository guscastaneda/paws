## A Few Things to Keep in Mind

**Peak season fills fast.** If your request overlaps a school vacation, holiday, or summer window, don't wait. A 50% deposit is required to hold peak season dates.

**[See peak season dates →]** → [[Pricing]]

**Adding a new dog to your family?** Reach out to us directly before submitting a request. New dogs go through the same Meet & Greet and trial process as all first-time visitors. → [[Request a Meet & Greet]]

**Can't find this page?** If you lost your booking link, contact us and we'll resend it right away. → [[Contact]]
